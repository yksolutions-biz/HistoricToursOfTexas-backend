Our Story

For over 25 years, Historic Tours of Texas has been sharing the rich tapestry of Lone Star history with visitors from around the world. Founded by Dr. Margaret Thompson, a renowned Texas historian, our company brings the past to life through expert storytelling and authentic experiences.

Our certified guides are passionate historians who dedicate themselves to preserving and sharing Texas's diverse heritage. From indigenous cultures and Spanish colonization to the fight for independence and the oil boom, we cover every chapter of Texas's remarkable story.
