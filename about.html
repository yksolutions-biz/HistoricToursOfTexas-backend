About Historic Tours of Texas

Discover our passion for preserving and sharing Texas's rich historical heritage

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Historic Tours of Texas began with a simple mission: to bring the rich tapestry of Texas history to life for visitors and locals alike. What started as a small operation offering tours of the Alamo has grown into the premier historical tour company in the Lone Star State.

Our team of expert historians and guides are passionate about Texas history, with backgrounds ranging from university professors to museum curators. We believe that history isn't just about dates and facts‚Äîit's about the stories, the people, and the places that shaped our great state.
